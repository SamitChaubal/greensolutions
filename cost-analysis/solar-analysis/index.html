Green Energy Home and our mission:

Solar PV Panels or PV Cells:

  • Cost: $10,000 to $30,000 (average for residential installation)
  • Additional equipment: 20-30% of total cost
  • Sales taxes: 5-10% of total cost

Contractor Labor Costs:

  • Labor: $2,000 to $5,000 (average for residential installation)
  • Permitting fees: Few hundred to over $1,000
  • Inspection costs: $100 to $500
  • Developer fees: Varies widely

Balance-of-System Equipment

  • Inverters: $1,000 to $5,000
  • Mounting equipment: $500 to $2,000
  • Wiring: $500 to $1,500

Energy Storage Devices (for systems installed after December 31, 2022):

  • Cost: $5,000 to $15,000 (average for residential installation)
  • Additional installation costs: 20-30% of total cost
