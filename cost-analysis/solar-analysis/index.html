How do solar panels work and who should use them:


Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electricity through the photovoltaic effect. When sunlight hits the panels, it energizes electrons in the semiconductor material, generating a flow of electricity. This electricity can be used to power homes, businesses, and communities, providing a clean and sustainable energy source. Anyone looking to reduce their reliance on traditional energy sources and lower their carbon footprint can benefit from using solar panels.
